Pet Sitting Rates

Need a sitter? Let us take care of it!

We offer top quality pet care in your home or ours.

Each day includes morning and evening walks, food and love for your pets. We’ll administer medicine, take out the garbage, bring in the paper and water the plants.

Alternate care arrangements can be made, we always our best to accommodate your needs and schedule.

Rates:

Pet Sitting (no run)

  • 3+ nights   $55 / night
  • 1-2 nights   $65 / night
    •

Pet Sitting (with a run a day while you're away)

  • 3+ nights   $79 / night
  • 1-2 nights   $89 / night
    •

Overnight pet sitting rates are based on check in by 8pm and check out at approximately 7am.

Please note: if you have an active running subscription at the time of pet sitting the “no run” rates will apply.

More than one dog? Add up to three extra dogs living in the same house for $5-$20 extra per night. We can handle up to three cats at no extra charge. Drop-off and pick up available. Rates vary depending on your need for pet sitting in our home.

Payment:

Payment is due before services are provided. You will be invoiced using Quickbooks, or by mail if you prefer. We accept cash, check, credit, debit, and PayPal.
