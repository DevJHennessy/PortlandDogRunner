We Love Running Dogs!

We are runners and dog lovers here to help make your life easier and your dog’s life happier and healthier. Our main focus is running, but some dogs are walkers and puppies need some play time before they are ready to run. When your dog has the exercise that they need, their home behavior, health and the overall happiness of your household can also improve!

Exercise for your dog will help with the release of endorphins, serotonin, and other natural chemicals, enabling your dog to better deal with stressful situations. This reaction to the joy of running causes dogs to take on an overall relaxed, happier and healthier attitude.

You will notice the difference immediately. Your dog’s stresses (such as separation anxiety when you go to work, not having any sheep to herd, being forced to live with cats, not getting to eat steak bites every day, etc.) will diminish as you realize more enjoyment and peace of mind in your life!
